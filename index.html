What's a housing choice voucher?

The housing choice voucher program is a federal program for providing financial assistance to low-income families, the elderly, and the disabled to afford safe, sanitary, and decent homes. You can find out more here.

How does DwellWell work?

DwellWell makes the process for taking advantage of housing choice voucher program easy. Simply create an account, answer a few questions, and select a home that fits your budget.
